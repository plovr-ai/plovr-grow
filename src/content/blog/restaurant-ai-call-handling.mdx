Every unanswered phone call at a restaurant represents a guest making a decision without you. Most of the time, that decision is made in favor of your competitor down the block.

## The math

Across 100 Localgrow launch partners, we measured the raw call recovery rate against the average ticket size of each location. The median restaurant was losing **$15,000 to $25,000 per month** in orders that never converted.

## Why phones still matter

Even in 2024, phone calls make up **34% of all booking-intent touches** in hospitality. Ignoring them is not a strategy — it\u2019s a leak.

## What to measure

- Calls answered under two rings
- Calls that resulted in a booking
- Average lead time from call to dining time

These three metrics tell you more about the health of your floor than almost anything else.
